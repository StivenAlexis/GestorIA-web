Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026.

GestorIA ("we") operates a centralized artificial intelligence and automation engine (multitenant SaaS) that client businesses use to serve their own end users through messaging channels such as WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger, and to generate and publish social media content on their behalf. This policy explains what data we process through that service and how we handle it.

1. Data collected through messaging channels

The content of messages an end user sends to a client business; end-user identifiers needed to reply through the same channel (WhatsApp number, Instagram/Messenger ID); conversation history; and client business configuration data (brand, tone, catalog) used to personalize replies and content. We do not collect data from people who do not directly interact with these channels.

2. How data is used

To generate automatic replies using third-party language models (Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude); to keep conversation context across messages from the same user; and to generate and schedule marketing content on behalf of the client business, subject to its own review and approval before publishing.

3. Who data is shared with

We do not sell data to third parties. Data is shared only with the infrastructure providers needed to operate the service: Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore (storage), Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude (response and content generation), and Meta Platforms, Inc. (message delivery via WhatsApp Business Platform, Messenger, and Instagram).

4. Multitenant isolation

Each client business can access only its own data. API access is protected by an individual key per business, and conversation data is logically isolated per business within our database.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain conversation history for as long as the client business keeps the service active, in order to provide context for future replies. Any end user, or the client business on their behalf, may request deletion of their data by writing to contacto@gestoriacore.com.ar. We process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Security

API access is protected via key-based authentication, and communications with messaging providers are validated through the signature mechanisms provided by each platform (for example, verifying Meta webhook signatures). All communication travels encrypted over HTTPS.

7. About this website

This website does not collect personal data: it has no forms that submit information to a server, and no third-party analytics or tracking scripts. The only contact mechanism is opening WhatsApp or the visitor's own email client; no data is processed or stored by the site itself.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy: contacto@gestoriacore.com.ar
